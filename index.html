About Me

Hi, I'm Suvetha Kugathas, a UX/UI designer based in Toronto. I'm currently a student at Humber College studying multimedia design and development. My passion for design has always been with me. Throughout the years, I explored various design platforms, where I learned to animate, video edit, graphic design, and create illustrations. Through my program, I found my passion for UX/UI design because I loved the journey of interacting with people and creating designs that will help them gain a positive experience with the product and brand itself.

As a designer, I'm interested in how to create experiences that are impactful, intuitive, and delightful. My design practice so far includes product and visual design, research, branding, and graphic design. I also enjoy motion design and video editing.

In my free time, I like playing video games, dancing and annoying my dog Rio the Shiba Inu. I also enjoy watching anime and sitcoms.
